My Favourite Travel Destinations

Every once in a while, I decide to take a break from everything and go on a vacation. I love to travel when on vacation, and these are some of my favourite travel destinations!

  • PARIS

    The Capital city of France (and the most populous), Paris is home to the Louvre Museum and the Eiffel Tower.
    When it comes to food, Paris is famous for its baguettes, croissants, and other delicious pastries.
    Paris is also known as the City of Love (and Romance), the Fashion Capital, and the City of Lights. One visit and you'll know for sure where these intriguing names come from!


    Click here to learn more about Paris!

  • EGYPT

    Egypt is regarded as the oldest tourism destination in the world, with a rich past that dates back to the beginning of civilisation. Travelers have been captivated by its majestic temples and pyramids for countless years.
    Although the majority of tourists visit Egypt to see the historical structures, there are also many natural attractions. The coral reefs and beach resorts along the Red Sea coast are well-known. One of my best experiences was reaching a cool freshwater spring oasis after a trek through The Sahara.


    Check out 10 best places to visit in Egypt!

  • DUBAI

    Ever seen the record-breaking Burj Khalifa with your own eyes? The breath-taking Palm? Dubai is home to these and many more beautiful attractions! Definitely makes a trip to the UAE worth it. My recommendation is to get a view of the city from the observation deck of the tallest building in the world.
